Skills:

Audio Engineering - I have about five months of experience in working with digital audio work stations and have fundamental knowledge about compression, mixing and mastering, and sound design. I'm currently learning mixing and mastering tracks from sources such as Youtube or asking my producer friends for advice. This is because having these skills can be extremely beneficial since many music producers require mixing and mastering services and I can easily make money by selling my skills.

Music Composing and Production - Experience using DAWS, primarily FL Studio, to arrange and master original music in genres such as trap, orchestra, and acoustic. I take alot of inspiration of Joe Hisaishi who is a Japanese composer known for his soundtracks in films like Spirited Away and Howls Moving Castle. Part of the reason I learned piano was because I was inspired by his music

Self Taught Music Theory and Piano Experience - I have about two years of experience in piano and possess intermediate music theory knowledge. Furthermore I have developed my ear to the point of instant relative pitch and am capable of playing most songs on the piano within minutes of listening to them. This ability can be useful in several musical environments; for example if I had the chance to accompany an orchestra or band, all I need to do is listen to the other instruments and I would be able to compliment them without having to rely on sheet music. I feel that musicians these days, especially those that received formal education rely too much on sheet music to songs. It take away from their creative liberty and forces them to play a certain way and in the style of the arranger. While I agree that being able to read sheet music is highly beneficial, it's more important to have listening and improvising skills that are just as adequate since music is about being creative

Sheet Music Transcription - This is something I don't do as often anymore but I can write sheet music using my ability to hear songs and play it on piano. For example, if there was a song that somebody wanted to play and they learn only by reading sheet music, I can write it for them after figuring out the notes of the song. Typically I use a program called MuseScore and have only done this a couple of times but it's a skill that be useful if I wanted to make some quick money by selling sheets to people that want to play their favorite songs

Video Editing - I have experience editing videos with Adobe Premiere Pro and After Effects. During the pandemic, I taught myself how to use these programs to a certain extent because my friends wanted their video game recordings to be edited.

Photo Editing and Manipulation- I have beginner experience using photo editing softwares such as Photoshop and Lightroom since I wanted to edit photos that a friend had taken; I also compliment this with a decent understanding of color grading and color theory.

Typing Speed- I can type up to 145-170WPM consistently with decent accuracy, although it depends on the content and the website I'm typing on.

Blender- In the past I've made several projects using Blender, which is a 3D rendering software used by many content creators and professionals.
