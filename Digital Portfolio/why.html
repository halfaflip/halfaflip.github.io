Why?

This Digital Portfolio is important for several reasons. Foremost, it taught me so many HTML and CSS techniques that I would never have learned without actually applying them. I knew nothing about CSS before making this portfolio, but after attempting to make portfolio with no experience, I learned more than I thought I could. I would look at guides in w3schools and constantly look up CSS properties that were new to me and then applied them to my portfolio so that I could reinforce it into my brain. If there's any proof of my progress over the past few weeks, it's the difference between my J1002 Journal and this website. It's also important because it gives me an opportunity to display my skills and background information; since this is for an assignment for a class I can treat it as informal. However, creating this portfolio gives me the experience to make a formal and professional resume/website if the time calls for it. Furthermore it gives me another place to store information from my resume if I need it in the future
